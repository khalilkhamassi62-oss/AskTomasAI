# Target Users

Two distinct personas use AskTomas for the same reason — compressed execution — but with different relationships to the system.

## Persona A – The Idea Holder

**USES ASKTOMAS TO**
- Turn ideas into live products
- Skip the hiring process entirely
- Understand their own business
- Launch in days, not months

The Idea Holder has a clear problem to solve, no technical background, thinks in outcomes (“I want a SaaS that does X”). They need heavy guidance, full automation, and zero friction. They trust the system to make expert decisions.

## Persona B – The SaaS Founder

**USES ASKTOMAS TO**
- 10x output without hiring
- Automate repetitive engineering
- Maintain architectural control
- Run parallel workstreams

The SaaS Founder is technically literate, has shipped before, knows what they want. They use AskTomas to compress execution time, not replace judgment. They will override decisions, tweak configs, and deep‑dive on the Agile board.
