# Vision & Positioning

**COMPANY IN A BOX**, **AI-NATIVE**, **SAAS FOUNDERS**

> "Give any founder a complete company — on demand, fully automated, running 24/7."

AskTomas is an AI-native platform that provides SaaS founders and idea holders with a fully autonomous, hierarchical company of AI agents — covering product, technology, marketing, and operations — that executes their vision end‑to‑end while they maintain strategic control through a Human‑in‑the‑Loop approval layer.

The product's central interface is **Tomas** — a CEO‑level orchestrator AI the user converses with in plain language. Behind Tomas sits an org chart of ~100–300 specialized micro‑agents, each an expert in a narrow domain, dispatching work to each other through a structured hierarchy modeled after a real company.

## The Core Promise

- **Full company structure** – CTO, CMO, CPO, CFO and their full teams — all AI agents.
- **Execution while you sleep** – Agents work asynchronously. You wake up to results, not todo lists.
- **You stay in control** – Every high‑stakes action requires your approval before it executes.
- **Plug in your providers** – Connect GitHub, Stripe, Vercel, Resend, Cloudflare and more — agents use them natively.
